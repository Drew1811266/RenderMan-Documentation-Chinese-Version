Down Time

The time required to upgrade the job database depends on the number of existing jobs in the database and the CPU and I/O speed of the host. In our test, a job database with 700,000 jobs was upgraded in under 2 minutes; the host had 2.8GHz CPUs and a 7200 RPM HDD.
