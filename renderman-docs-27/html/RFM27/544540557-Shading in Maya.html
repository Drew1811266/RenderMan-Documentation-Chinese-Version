Shading in Maya

Source: https://rmanwiki-27.pixar.com/space/RFM27/544540557/Shading+in+Maya
Downloaded: 2026-06-15T03:49:12+00:00

Shading and Look Development in Maya is natural using Maya's built in look development tools, combined with Pixar's powerful BXDFs. Using a combination of Pixar and Maya patterns with the multitude of available BXDFs, any look can be achieved.

Pattern Nodes

  • Supported Native Maya Patterns

  • Pixar Patterns 

Displacement Nodes

  • PxrDisplace

  • PxrDispTransform

  • PxrDispScalarLayer

  • PxrDispVectorLayer

BXDF Nodes

  • PxrSurface

  • PxrLayeredSurface

  • PxrMarschnerHair

  • PxrVolume

  • MaterialX Lama

  • PxrConstant - single color or texture

  • PxrBlack - does not scatter/reflect light
