Cameras in Katana

Source: https://rmanwiki-27.pixar.com/space/RFK27/546178629/Cameras+in+Katana
Downloaded: 2026-06-15T03:49:12+00:00

RenderMan for Katana supports cameras created with a CameraCreate node or imported from an Alembic file. You can control various camera effects through the attributes on the camera using PrmanCameraSettings.  Supported camera effects include:

  • Motion Blur in Katana
  • Display & Sample Filters in Katana
  • Camera Settings in Katana
