RenderMan Project Based Learning

Ever wanted to know how RenderMan and Pixar artists create their inspiring imagery?

Well, you’re in luck … here’s a wide variety of projects to help you understand every aspect of how each image was made, complete with downloadable scenes for you to inspect, pick apart, and learn from.

Night sets across the land as the UFO-hunting van is out in the wilderness, looking and searching for his ultimate prize … to finally spot a UFO.
