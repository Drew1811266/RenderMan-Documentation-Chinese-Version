Project: RazorBack Whiptail

Created & Written by: Kurtis Dawe & Matteo LLorens

Edited by: Leif Pedersen

For some time now, I've been eager to learn how to use Mari software. When Kurtis Dawe offered several artists the opportunity to work on one of his assets, I immediately seized the chance. Being passionate about creatures and monsters since my introduction to 3D work, I enthusiastically immersed myself in this exciting project.

Access to such a high-quality asset enabled me to focus on practicing the main areas I wanted to refine: creature texturing and look-dev.
