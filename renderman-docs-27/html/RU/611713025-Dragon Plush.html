Scene by: Julia Boisvert, Evan Dickinson, Emmalie Hall

Overview

Dragon Plush is an asset look developed by artist Julia Boisvert and modeled by Evan Dickinson from a concept by Emmalie Hall for a group short titled Serendipity.

The multi-layered felt groom was developed in Houdini and rendered using RenderMan and LamaHairChiang shaders. The character consists of a variety of materials and hair types created using a mixture of Substance Painter, Houdini, and RenderMan for Maya.

.

About the Artist

Julia Boisvert is a Visualization student at Texas A&M University with interests in look and visual development. She enjoys creating environment-centered 2D art and is currently interested in further exploring character and groom-driven look development.
