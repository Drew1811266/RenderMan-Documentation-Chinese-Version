Everyone knows what Black and White colors mean in rendering, right?

Of course!

Well …. just in case, here’s a quick guide on how RenderMan treats Black and White values when it comes to Roughness, Metallic, and Bump.

Material Roughness

Black = Shiny

Roughness Value: 1

White = Rough

Roughness Value: 0

Material Metallic Workflow

Black = Non-Metal

Roughness Value: 1

White = Metal

Roughness Value: 0

Bump Maps

Black = Bump In

White = Bump Out

Displacement Maps

Black = Displace Inwards

White = Displace Outwards

Presence (Opacity)

Black = OFF

White = ON
