Overview

This HDRI collection was captured at Stinson Beach, California, which was the visual inspiration for Pixar's short film Piper. This collection was used while developing the look for the film.

Legend tells of Loren Carpenter showing up serendipitously at the beach right as the camera rig broke...and of course he happened to be walking with tools to fix it at the crack of dawn...

How-to

Drag the contents of the Materials folder into your Asset Library > EnvironmentMaps folder. The files are organized by category and can be easily searched in your preset browser.

Make sure to transfer the installation RenderManAssetLibrary folder to a location with full writing privileges.

Once you transfer the Materials folder and move your Asset Library into a different location, it is important to tell Maya through the preferences that you want to use the new path.

Or you can create a new environment variable in your Maya.env file, which will make sure you are always pointing to the right folder:

RMAN_ASSET_LIBRARY = /My/Folder/Example/RenderManAssetLibrary
