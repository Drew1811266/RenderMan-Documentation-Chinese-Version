Overview

These are some light maps shot in the studio and edited as HDRI textures.

Texturing your lights is a good way to have non-perfect reflections and shadows. You can plug them into the color map attribute of your PxrRectLight. Textures are preconverted in tex format and available in an easy drag-and-drop folder for your RenderMan Preset Browser.

Enjoy!

  • The soft box is entirely neutral, desaturated. Original size 65 x 65 cm.

  • The spot is balanced at 6500K with minor warm variations. Original size is 18 cm in diameter.

  • The kitchen is shot at 6500K, giving a warm color. Original size is 36 x 6 cm.

  • The kitchen is shot with a white balance set on the light source. Original size is 36 x 6 cm.

Note that you should use high resolution versions for closeups only, as low resolution versions should be good enough for most cases.

How-to

Drag the contents into your Asset Library > LightRigs folder. The files are organized by category and can be easily searched in your preset browser.

Make sure to transfer the installation RenderManAssetLibrary folder to a location with full writing privileges.

Once you transfer the Materials folder and move your Asset Library into a different location, it is essential to tell Maya through the preferences that you want to use the new path.

Or you can create a new environment variable in your Maya.env file, which will make sure you are always pointing to the correct folder:

RMAN_ASSET_LIBRARY = /My/Folder/Example/RenderManAssetLibrary
