Shading Treasure

The gold treasure material is a very simple shader.

A Voronoi procedural driving reflective roughness attributes drive its textural detail.

The pattern setup uses a large-scale Voronoi noise, connected to the roughness gain component; this provides a rich textural quality that adds a dirty, ancient feel to the treasure.
