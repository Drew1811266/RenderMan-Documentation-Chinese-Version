An introduction to the new RenderMan for Blender plugin. Details include rendering to RIS and XPU, the impressive Bump to Roughness node, PBR Metallic Workflow, and much more!

It's time to get some rendering going with this Kickstarter. I’ll take you through the process of starting with a base scene, adding some shaders, introducing lights, and showing you the preset browser. We’ll finish it off with a final render.
