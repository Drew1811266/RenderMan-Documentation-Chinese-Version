Live Stats in Solaris

Source: https://rmanwiki-27.pixar.com/space/RFH27/544642250/Live+Stats+in+Solaris
Downloaded: 2026-06-15T03:49:12+00:00



Live Stats in Solaris allows you to inspect your Solaris scene.

To begin using Live Stats, you first need to attach the stats panel to a Render.  Simply press Attach Render and when you begin rendering the data will appear.
