Frequently Asked Questions

  • Who developed RfH? 
    This is co-developed between SideFx and Pixar's RenderMan group.

  • How often is RfH sync up with a RenderMan release?
    We try to sync as soon as a RenderMan release is available. For big releases, such as a  major version change (not a point release), it will take time especially when a new feature UI is not yet supported in Houdini.

  • How do I report bugs and suggestions? 
    Please post on RenderMan for Houdini Forum.

  • When I am not sure if I have a Houdini or RenderMan bug, where should I report it? 
    First take a look here and then, if it is RenderMan shaders and output related, please log a bug on the RenderMan for Houdini Forum.
