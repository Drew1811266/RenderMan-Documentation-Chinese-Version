See  Depth of Field for RenderMan depth of field.


To enable depth of filed, turn on RenderMan RIS ROP's Rendering | Depth Of Field: Enable Depth Of Field.


To change the camera properties such as Focus Distance and F-Stop, adjust them in the Camera's Sampling Tab as well as in the RenderMan controls for the added properties like Bokeh.
