Getting Help

Source: https://rmanwiki-27.pixar.com/space/RFH27/544642900/Getting+Help
Downloaded: 2026-06-15T03:49:12+00:00



  • RenderMan help: See RenderMan.
  • To get help on the context, hover the mouse over an item such as a parameter to see its tooltip.
  • To get help on a shelf tool, hover the mouse over a tool and hit F1.
  • Additional resources are linked in the RenderMan Menu

For commercial support, take a quick look at our Support Page or visit the commercial forum.
