Projection Plugins

Source: https://rmanwiki-27.pixar.com/space/REN27/542231364/Projection+Plugins
Downloaded: 2026-06-15T03:49:12+00:00


PRMan has the ability to create custom RixProjection plugins. These plugins can be used to characterize camera and lens projections. The distribution includes several built-in plugins, as well. Note that whether or not the depth of field and bokeh settings apply to projection plugins depends on the individual plugin. Below we explain the most common and full-featured projection plugin.

  • PxrCamera

  • PxrPanini
